Fall Safari Activities and Wagon Rides (Seasonal)

Fall time is park owners Big Joel and Mimi's favorite time of the year. During this time of the year the park is decorated for the Halloween and Fall season, transforming Big Joel's Safari into Big Joel's Spooky Safari! Beginning at the end of September and operating regular days of operation until Halloween. During this time we offer wagon rides that operate every day we are open. We take you one full round in our old hay wagon with our trusty old tractor in our large field which is home most of our deer, elk, bison, and watusi cattle as well as our many ducks and geese. During the ride our ride operator will inform you about our animal residents and facts about Big Joel's Safari. Tickets are $2.50 per rider (under 2 years of age are free)
