We are partially wheelchair accessible. The zoo is on natural terrain, which means there are slight inclines, gravel, and grass to manage. Some chairs do better than others and we not have wheelchair rentals.

You can use your hands, spoons, or special feed tubes to feed the animals (but don't use your cup they might steal or break it!)
